HIGH-END WHISKY

A WORLD OF FLAVOURS

Visualization of a complex and delicate taste sensation. A series of 6 compositions visually describing the range of flavours of high-end whisky. From round and floral to a more rich and smokey taste experience.

CREATIVE CONCEPT · IMAGE CREATION · BRANDING
