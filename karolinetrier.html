KAROLINE TRIER

SUSTAINABLE FOOD CULTURE

Karoline Trier runs a food school for kids and adults. Her aim is to make the world a better place. She does that by sharing her knowledge on how to make good, healthy, and sustainable food. She believes that good food is a culture we pass on. Education of new generations is how we start a more conscious way of eating.

IDENTITY

Sharing knowledge and inspiration is key to Karoline Trier’s stationary. We designed 12 business cards, one for each month.
The colour on the card represents that month’s in-season ingredient. On the back, we added a recipe featuring the ingredient.
