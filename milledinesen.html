MILLE DINESEN

LIVING A BALANCED LIFE

Mille Dinesen is a Danish actor, who, in her youth, experienced a life full of stress. She changed her lifestyle and focused on becoming more balanced and healthy by nursing her mind and body. Her organic luxury inside our beauty series is the result of many years of experimenting and tells the story of her own journey towards creating a balanced life for herself. The products are made of pure organic and natural ingredients.
