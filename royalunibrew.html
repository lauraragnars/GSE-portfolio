Royal Unibrew

BE CURIOUS

DDB and Royal Unibrew created the product Tempt Cider together. At first glance the design looks sweet and innocent but when you look closer you’ll find little intriguing secrets. When you looked through the small keyhole on the back label on the bottles, you could read the hidden message: I have a secret - temptcider.com At the website you could read and post your own secrets. Tempt Cider is made for the curious.
