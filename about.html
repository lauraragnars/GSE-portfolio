ABOUT

Gry Strange Echwald is a multi-disciplinary creative based in Copenhagen.

She helps brands transform and grow their businesses through storytelling, art direction, design and creativity.

Creating authentic brand identity and engaging communication since 2003.

She is educated from the Graphic Arts Institute of Denmark and works across the fields of culture, art, design, photography and beauty.

She works closely and respectfully with her clients, independently or in small or larger teams tailored to the client’s needs.
