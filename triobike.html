TRIO BIKE

CLIMATE-FRIENDLY TRANSPORTATION

Trio Bike is a Danish cargo bike company that wanted to challenge the way we transport ourselves. Can we make people change their behaviour and choose bikes over cars?
They needed a rebranding and repositioning of the Trio Bike brand. We created a platform where we invited people to “Live the Trio Way” in an easy-going, vibrant and playful manner.
